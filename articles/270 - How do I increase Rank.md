# How do I increase Rank?

## Rank is determined by the total sum of all the car levels in the garage.

##

For example, if you have a car that is level 4 and another car that is level 5, then your calculated rank would be 9.

To increase your rank, increase the level of a car. To increase the level of a car, complete games. The experience points you earn will increase the level of that car.
