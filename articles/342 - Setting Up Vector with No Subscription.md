# Setting Up Vector with No Subscription

### I don't have a subscription, but I need to set up my Vector!

Digital Dream Labs is aware of an issue where, in the mobile app for Vector, users may not be able to complete the setup steps outlined in the app without a paid subscription. To work around this issue while Digital Dream Labs works to address it, please use the following steps:

 1. Click "Tips for Speaking"
 2. Click "Skip Section"

**Note: These steps may need to be repeated through multiple sections in setup.**
