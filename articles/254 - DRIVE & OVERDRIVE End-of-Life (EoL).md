# DRIVE & OVERDRIVE End-of-Life (EoL)

### Product End-Of-Life Schedule

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| App Name | Platform | End-Of-Life Date |
| Anki DRIVE | - Android - iOS - Kindle | November 17, 2017 |
| Anki OVERDRIVE | - Android - iOS - Kindle | October 15, 2021 |
| Anki OVERDRIVE: Fast & Furious Edition | - Android - iOS - Kindle | October 15, 2021 |

---

On November 17, 2017, we officially ended support of the Drive app and related hardware. Official end-of-life for the Anki OVERDRIVE and OVERDRIVE: Fast and Furious Edition occurred on October 15th, 2021. While we realize End-of-Life for any product or app can be unwelcome news, we do want to be clear on how this will impact you.

**Please Note:** Digital Dream Labs is still able to support [Car Repairs](https://www.digitaldreamlabs.com/products/repair-center) for most vehicles to get the most out of your DRIVE and OVERDRIVE hardware. We also provide [car tires](https://www.digitaldreamlabs.com/products/overdrive-tires) alone if you do not need full car refurbishment.

---

### Replacing Anki Drive, OverDrive, and OverDrive: Fast & Furious

We now have OverDrive 2.6 available for use with cars from the Drive, OverDrive, and OverDrive: Fast & Furious kits so you can keep racing!

---

**For Anki DRIVE, we no longer:**

- Maintain or evolve the DRIVE app or provide any support for device compatibility.
- Offer any active technical support for the DRIVE app or any related hardware.
- Stock replacement/piece part items such as Cars, Tracks, Tires for sale.

**For Anki OVERDRIVE, we no longer:**

- Maintain or evolve the OVERDRIVE app or provide any support for device compatibility, except for OverDrive 2.6.

**For Anki OVERDRIVE: Fast & Furious Edition, we no longer:**

- Maintain or evolve the OVERDRIVE Fast & Furious Edition app or provide any support for device compatibility.
- Offer any active technical support for the OVERDRIVE Fast & Furious Edition app or any edition-specific hardware like track pieces.
