# Where can I purchase tires and other spare parts?

Learn below where to get spare tires.

---

Consumableparts, such as tires, are available from the Digital Dream Labs site. Currently, DDL offers tires only, which are available [here](https://www.digitaldreamlabs.com/products/overdrive-tires).

---

## Batteries

**Vehicle parts including batteries are not user serviceable and therefore not available.** Please contact us via email at support@digitaldreamlabs.com for troubleshooting cars or, if there is a known battery issue you may purchase a [Car Repair](https://www.digitaldreamlabs.com/products/repair-center).
