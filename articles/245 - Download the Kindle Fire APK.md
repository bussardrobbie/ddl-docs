# Download the Kindle Fire APK

If you are unable to locate or download the OVERDRIVE or OVERDRIVE: Fast & Furious Edition (Available September 2017) from within the Amazon Appstore, this means that your Amazon Kindle device is known to not support OVERDRIVE and will not support OVERDRIVE: Fast & Furious Edition apps. Either it does not meet a specific requirement, or we’ve have specifically disabled it because there were problems that we could not fix or workaround.
