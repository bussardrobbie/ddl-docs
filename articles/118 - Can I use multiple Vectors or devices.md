# Can I use multiple Vectors or devices?

## I have more than 1 Vector

- If you have more than 1 Vector, **it is possible to link your account to them all**
- If you only have 1 device, before using a different Vector, you will need to disconnect from the robot you are already connected to by opening the **Vector app > Settings > Disconnect**
- Digital Dream Labs is creating **a world where robots have relationships with people**. As such, Vector will initially not communicate with other Vectors. Nor will he communicate with Cozmo or Pets
- Vector will sync to the **closest cube**

## I have more than 1 device

- If you have more than one device, **it is possible** to log into your account on both devices but if both devices have the Vector app open and in the foreground, both will try to connect to Vector
- Since only 1 device can be associated with 1 Vector at any time, you will need to **close the Vector app on one of your devices**
