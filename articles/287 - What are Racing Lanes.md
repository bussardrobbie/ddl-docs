# What are "Racing Lanes"?

OVERDRIVE and OVERDRIVE: Fast & Furious Edition include a "Racing Lanes" option. Read more about this feature in this article.

---

By default, you will have "Racing Lanes" activated. This means you will have 4 lanes and will need to rotate your device to change lanes (once per lane).

For a more dynamic driving experience, you can simply disable this option by going to the Settings Menu and unticking "Racing Lanes". By disabling this setting, you will need fewer device tilts to change between 16 lanes.
