# Why is there no sound?

If you're not hearing any sounds from Vector, please:

1. [**Reboot Vector**](https://support.anki.com/hc/en-us/articles/360009804394-How-to-reset-erase-and-restore-Vector)
2. Adjust the volume on Vector. To do this: open the Vector app > tap Settings. If the volume is already on med or high, adjust it anyway to reset the volume
