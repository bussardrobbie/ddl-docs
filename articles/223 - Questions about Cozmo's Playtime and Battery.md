# Questions about Cozmo's Playtime and Battery

Cozmo has an internal Lithium-ion battery that is charged using the included charging dock connected to a powered USB outlet.

---

Typical charging times for Cozmo are approximately 30 minutes, and will vary based upon the USB power source that you use.

---

When fully charged, that allows Cozmo to play for approximately 45 minutes. Actual playtime will vary based upon how you use Cozmo.

---

Cozmo's internal battery is not user serviceable.
