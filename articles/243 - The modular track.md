# The modular track

## How does the modular track work?

- Modular track pieces connect via magnets on each end
- There are guides that allow you to quickly align each piece
- The Starter Kit includes 10 track pieces of various shapes that will allow you to build [eight different track configurations](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/282-the-8-basic-tracks)
- You can also add expansion track pieces
- Once the track is created, your car will need to do a few laps to learn the track

---

## Durability of the track

- The tracks are made out of Polypropylene and very flexible
- While the track pieces might not break, bending or folding them can irreparably damage the track and negatively affect game play
