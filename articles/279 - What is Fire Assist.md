# What is "Fire Assist"?

This is a quick explanation of the Fire Assist setting and how it works.

---

Fire Assist helps players who are unfamiliar with how different weapon types utilize different firing mechanisms.

---

With the Fire Assist setting enabled,

- 'tap to fire' weapons will auto-fire with 2 seconds between shots
- 'release to fire' weapons will auto-release at max power when holding the fire button

Fire Assist does not impact 'Deployed' or 'Machine Gun' weapons and it is on by default.
