# What's the difference between Experience Points and Coins?

## Experience Points

Are earned by playing. The points are accumulated by the Supercar or Supertruck you use and as you earn more points, the level of the vehicle increases. The more you level up your vehicle, the more Experience Points you will need to earn to reach the next level.

## Coins

Is the virtual currency used in OVERDRIVE. You earn coins in-game by completing games and cannot purchase them with real currency. Spend coins in the Coin Shop on new Weapons, Special Items and Upgrades for your vehicles. Complete Goals to earn extra coins.
