# What is "Pro Speed"?

OVERDRIVE and OVERDRIVE: Fast&Furious Edition include a "Pro Speed" option that allows you to change the top speed. Read more about this feature in this article.

---

By default, your app will have "Pro Speed" activated which means that your cars will be able to reach the highest-possible top speed that the combination of support items, game mode and Commander level allows.

If you feel that you'd prefer a lower top speed, you can simply deactivate this option by going to the Settings Menu and unticking "Pro Speed":
