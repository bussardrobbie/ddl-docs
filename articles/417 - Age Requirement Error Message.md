# Age Requirement Error Message

Digital Dream Labs collects dates of birth for some accounts in accordance with our [Privacy Policy](https://www.digitaldreamlabs.com/policies/privacy-policy) and to enforce our [Terms of Service](https://www.digitaldreamlabs.com/policies/terms-of-service). In some cases, while setting up Vector, an error may appear that states:

 **"You do not meet the age requirement to register Vector. Ask your parent or guardian to set up Vector."**

Two particular issues may cause this issue:

- Your Vector account has no date of birth set.
- The date of birth set in your Vector account does not meet the requirements of the [Terms of Service](https://www.digitaldreamlabs.com/policies/terms-of-service).

To resolve this issue, please Contact Us via email at support@digitaldreamlabs.com and we will be glad to assist.
