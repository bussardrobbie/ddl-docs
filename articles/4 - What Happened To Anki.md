# What Happened To Anki?

Digital Dream Labs acquired the rights to Cozmo, Vector, and Overdrive in December 2019 after Anki lost a critical round of funding in April of 2019. Anki shut its doors for good a month later, after arranging for its products to be supported for a short while after Anki's closure.

Digital Dream Labs is now in the process of relaunching Cozmo, Vector, and Overdrive with new features and improvements!
