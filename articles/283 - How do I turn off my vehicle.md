# How do I turn off my vehicle?

This FAQ explains how to **turn off** your Supercar or Supertruck. Please note that **powercycling** or **resetting** the vehicle is not possible.

---

## Where is the Power Button?

- You will find the grey power button **on the underneath** of each Supercar and Supertruck

## What does the Power button do?

- Pressing the power button once turns the vehicle off or on
- Placing the vehicle on the included charging platform also turns it on
- Pressing the power button 5 times in rapid succession puts the vehicle in Drone Mode. Placing the vehicle on the track in Drone Mode can help test vehicle performance
