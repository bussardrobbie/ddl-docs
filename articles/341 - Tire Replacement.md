# Tire Replacement

**Tire Replacement**

This article briefly outlines the steps for physically replacing old Overdrive tires with new tires.

1. Hold car right side up, with the back of the car to the right (or left if you’re left handed)
2. Place forefinger and thumb on the bottom of the rear tire, apply gentle pressure and slide the tire off of the hub. Place aside.
3. Rotate the car so that the opposite rear tire is in front of you and then repeat Step 2.
4. Take one of the new tires and slip it on the rear hub facing you. Ensure that the tire is centered on the hub.
5. Rotate the car so that the opposite rear tire is in front of you and then repeat Step 4.
6. Test the car on the track to make sure that you are a screaming demon once again!
