# Compatible Devices

## ls my device compatible with Vector and the Vector Robot companion app?

Vector and the Vector Robot companion app are designed to work with popular modern devices.

##

Please Note: If your device cannot find and download the Vector Robot companion app in the Apple App Store, Google Play store, or Amazon Appstore, then your device will not work with Vector. Please read [here](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/85-compatible-devices-further-information) for more information.

##

When selecting a mobile device to use with Digital Dream Labs products, please consider the following important guidelines:

- Minimum requirements
- Validated devices
- Operating systems

##

Digital Dream Labs tests and publishes a list of devices with operating systems that we know to work well with Digital Dream Labs products. These lists are actively updated as we improve our products. Due to differences among devices, some devices that meet or exceed the minimum requirements may not work well with Vector.

---

## Minimum Requirements

### Operating System

- iOS 10
- Android 5 (Lollipop)
- Fire OS 5

### Hardware

- A WiFi internet router that broadcasts in at least 802.11n 2.4GHz channels

## If Vector is autonomous, why do I need to use a device with Vector? Why do I need the Vector Robot companion app?

##

While Vector is an autonomous robot (you can interact with him using your voice and he will automatically update himself when a software update is available), the Vector Robot companion app is required to:

- Successfully set up Vector
- Associating your Vector with your Anki Account
- Configuring Vector to join WiFi networks and setting basic settings
- Help you learn how to interact with Vector
- Manage Face Recognition and Photos
