## What is the general rule for device compatibility?

The vast majority of devices that can download our App from their respective app store are likely to support all aspects of Cozmo.

If you can use your device to search for, find, and download the App in the Apple App Store, the Google Play store, or the Amazon Appstore that device is very likely to be compatible with Cozmo.

Please be aware that because of the significant number of different models, variants, and manufacturing standards of available devices, DDL can only assure the compatibility of devices that we actively test internally. **A list of compatible devices can be found**[here](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/135-compatible-devices).

---

## What if my device cannot find and download the App?

You’ve used your device to search for, but cannot find and download the App from the Apple App Store, the Google Play store, or the Amazon Appstore locations.

This means that your device is known to not support Cozmo. Either it does not meet a specific requirement, or we’ve have specifically disabled it because there were problems that we could not fix or workaround.

## Huawei Lite Devices

Some Huawei "Lite" devices have performance and disconnection issues running the Cozmo app. Based upon our tests, we have determined that some of these devices are not compatible with the Cozmo app and have had to manually excluded them from installing the app via the Google Play Store.

## Sony Xperia Devices

Some Sony Xperia devices crash upon running the Cozmo app. Based upon our tests, we have determined that these devices are not compatible with the Cozmo app and have had to manually excluded some of them from installing the app via the Google Play Store.

**DDL apps are not supported running on devices that are:**

- Jailbroken, rooted, or running custom ROMs
- Running a Beta operating system
- Installed on external memory (SD Cards, USB, etc.)

**DDL apps are only supported on devices running officially released builds provided by their manufacturer and installed on internal memory.**

## What are the Minimum Requirements?

In general, for a device to potentially work, it needs to meet or exceed both of these Software and Hardware Requirements.

Software

- iOS 9
- Android 5 (Lollipop)
- Fire OS 5

## Is there anything else to try if a device cannot find the App in any app store?

If you are using an Android device or an Amazon Kindle Fire OS device and they are not listed anywhere on this page and your unable to download the Cozmo app from the app store, you can try using the [**COZMO: APK for Unsupported Devices**](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/228-download-the-android-kindle-fire-apk).

###

This .apk file will install on both Android and Fire OS devices.
